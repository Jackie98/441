About Jackie

Jackie Evans-Shaw is a Media Arts major with a concentration in Digital Art and Animation, and a minor in Linguistics.

When not in school, she is also a gamer, illustrator, astral world traveler and part-time sorcerer who enjoys exploring the natural world with her boyfriend and dog.

Originally from Bozeman, Jackie finds comfort in the familiarity of Missoula, and appreciates the new experiences and friends she meets along the way.

If all goes according to plan, she hopes to graduate in Fall of 2020.

My Interests

When she's not slaving over homework or designing for the Kaimin, Jackie can be found drawing, painting, reading about plants, or watching French movies. Some of her career goals include:

- Becoming a comic illustrator/video game concept artist and/or animator;

- Working with other linguists to research endangered languages;

- Operating at some point in her life within the Forest Service as a Forestry Aid

When she was young, Jackie wanted to be anything that tickled her fancy. As an adult, she has decided she will be all of those things.
